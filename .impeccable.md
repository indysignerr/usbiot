## Design Context

### Users
Families in Biot (Cote d'Azur, France) looking for a football club for their children (ages 5-18) or themselves (seniors). They browse on mobile (60%+) and desktop, often in the evening after work. Key jobs: find training schedules, register a child, contact the club, check match results.

### Brand Personality
Familial, chaleureux, sportif — a warm community club with premium infrastructure. Not corporate, not ultra-competitive. Think "village club that punches above its weight."

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Ferrari editorial precision meets Nike sporty energy — cinematic red/gold gradients, bold typography, sharp corners, generous whitespace
- **References**: Ferrari (premium red/gold, editorial layouts), Nike (sporty energy, bold CTAs), Cal.com (clean forms)
- **Anti-references**: Generic WordPress football sites, clipart-heavy club pages, dark-mode-only gamer aesthetics
- **Theme**: Light default + Dark mode. Light uses warm cream (#FAFAFA), dark uses ink (#0a0908)
- **Colors**: Rouge #d8261c (primary), Or #cdb728 (accent), Blanc #FAFAFA (bg), Ink #100C08 (text)
- **Typography**: Plus Jakarta Sans (headings) + DM Sans (body) — fluid clamp() scale
- **Motion**: Moderate — scroll reveals, hover effects, staggered page load. No scroll hijacking.

### Design Principles
1. **Familial but premium** — warm tone, professional execution
2. **Content-first** — every section earns its place, no filler
3. **Red with restraint** — red is powerful, use it strategically (heroes, CTAs, accents), not everywhere
4. **Gold marks importance** — gold highlights key actions and status markers
5. **Sharp, not rounded** — squared corners (rounded-none) for editorial feel, breaking the soft/friendly template look
